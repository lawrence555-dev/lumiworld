# Project Proposal: LumiWorld - Integrated Pre-K STEAM & Early Literacy Initiative

**Curriculum System:** US-Based (Aligned with US Common Core & NGSS)  
**Target Grade:** Pre-Kindergarten (Ages 4-5)  
**Unit Theme:** Sharing the Planet (Environmental Stewardship)  
**Format:** Web-Based Interactive Curriculum

---

## 1. Executive Summary
This initiative aims to support this semester's core inquiry unit by integrating **STEAM education** with **Early Literacy** through digital technology. LumiWorld is not just a game; it is a supplementary learning platform designed in accordance with CCSS (Common Core State Standards) and NGSS (Next Generation Science Standards).

Through an 8-week immersive experience, we transform abstract ecological concepts into concrete interactive tasks, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for students entering Kindergarten.

## 4. Pedagogical Approach
To meet the expectations of elite US educational systems, our design incorporates the following strategies:

### 1. Differentiated Instruction
Recognizing that not all 4-year-olds progress at the same rate, the system includes an **"Adaptive Engine"**:
- **Scaffolding:** When a student struggles with a math task, the system automatically reduces options (e.g., from 3 to 2) and provides visual hints rather than just giving the answer.

### 2. Phonemic Awareness & Sight Words (Science of Reading)
In the ELA modules, we emphasize **"Letter-Sound Correspondence"** rather than rote memorization.
- **Mechanism:** Students must hear the initial sound of "Fish" /f/ and find the letter 'F' to complete the puzzle. This aligns with the core principles of early reading development.

### 3. Social Emotional Learning (SEL)
Informed by the CASEL framework, we integrate empathy education in Weeks 7-8.
- When a dolphin is trapped in trash, it displays "Sad" emotions, changing to "Happy" after being cleaned. This teaches students to recognize emotions and cultivate empathy for the environment.

## 5. Assessment & Data Insights
The system provides **Formative Assessment** data similar to established educational reports:
- **Mastery Tracking:** Has the student mastered "Counting 1-20"? (Yes / In Progress / Needs Support)
- **Engagement Metrics:** Time spent on "Logical Sequencing" tasks, helping educators identify individual student interests and strengths.
